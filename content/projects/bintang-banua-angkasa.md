Bintang Banua Angkasa is a campus profile website developed for a higher education institution.

## Overview
A professional and structured campus profile website designed to present study programs, institutional information, news, announcements, and academic agendas in one accessible platform.
The website prioritizes user-friendly navigation, clean layout, and fast loading performance to ensure prospective students and visitors can easily explore campus offerings and stay updated.
Built and optimized to deliver reliability, accessibility, and strong online presence.

- WordPress
- Elementor
- SEO Optimization
- Caching Optimization
