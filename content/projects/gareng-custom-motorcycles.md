Gareng Custom Motorcycles is a lightweight link-in-bio style website built to showcase a custom motorcycle brand and provide quick access to important links.

## Overview

A minimal and fast landing page designed to centralize Gareng Custom Motorcycles' online presence.

The website focuses on simplicity, performance, and clear call-to-action buttons directing users to social media, marketplace, and contact channels.

Built as a fully static site for optimal speed and reliability.

## Stack

- Astro
- TailwindCSS
- Cloudflare Pages
