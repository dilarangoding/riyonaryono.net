PPDB SMK Telekomunikasi Telesandi Bekasi is an online student admission system developed to handle digital registration and payment processing.

## Overview

A web-based admission platform built to streamline the student enrollment process for one academic year.

The system was successfully used by approximately 350+ students during the admission period.

Key functionalities include:

* Online student registration
* Online payment processing
* Digital form submission
* Admission data management
* Information publishing for applicants

The platform replaced manual registration workflows and improved efficiency, accessibility, and data organization.

## Stack

* Laravel
* Bootstrap
* MySQL
* Payment Integration

## Project Info

Type: Institutional Project

Users: 350+ Students (Single Academic Year)
